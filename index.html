Gymnasium

The newly opened South Forsyth Gymnasium is now available for the public to use as a place for event oraganization. It has a maximum capacity of 3500 people. The new SFHS gymnaisum also includes a lot of space for easy acces to seats and many different locations to enter from. There is about 2000 parking spots at the location itself, and can book a couple hundered more from certain shops around the general vicinity. The gymnasium is also very close to the Collections Mall at Forsyth, which is a exceptional place to go after an event for a variety of things. Booking the South Forsyth gymansium is very affordable and it is the pinnicale of a great experience. We hope you will enjoy your experience at the South Forsyth gymnasium.
